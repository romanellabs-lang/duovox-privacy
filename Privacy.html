Privacy Policy – DuoVox

Last updated: 02 May 2026

1. Who we are

DuoVox is a mobile application that provides real-time voice translation services.

For the purposes of UK GDPR, DuoVox acts as a data controller only for limited on-device processing described in this policy.

2. Data we collect

2.1 No personal data collected

  • No names, emails, or accounts are collected
  • No voice recordings are stored or transmitted
  • No translation history is stored on servers

2.2 On-device processing

Voice input and translation processing occur locally on the device and are not sent to external servers.

3. Legal basis (UK GDPR)

Processing is based on Article 6(1)(b) – performance of a service requested by the user.

4. Microphone usage

The microphone is used only for real-time speech-to-text translation.

No audio is recorded or stored.

5. Advertising

The app uses third-party advertising services provided by Google AdMob.

  • Advertising ID
  • Device information
  • Approximate location
  • Ad interaction data

This data is managed by Google under their own privacy policy.

Google Privacy Policy

6. Legal basis for ads

Ads are shown based on user consent (where required) and legitimate interest for app sustainability.

7. Third-party services

  • Google AdMob
  • Google Play Services

8. Data retention

No personal data is stored or retained by DuoVox.

9. Security

All processing occurs locally on the device. No server-side storage is used.

10. User rights

Under UK GDPR, users have rights to access, rectify, or delete personal data. As no data is stored, these rights mainly apply to third-party services like Google.

11. Children’s privacy

DuoVox is not intended for children under 13 without supervision.

12. Changes

This policy may be updated periodically. Changes will be posted here.
