Privacy Policy – DuoVox

Last updated: June 9, 2026

DuoVox respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Data Collection

DuoVox does not collect personal information such as names, email addresses, contacts, photos, or user-generated content.

However, the app may use third-party advertising services that automatically collect certain device identifiers and advertising-related information.

2. Audio Usage

The app uses your device's microphone only to convert speech into text and provide translation functionality. Audio is not stored by DuoVox.

3. Translation

Translations are processed locally on your device whenever possible. DuoVox does not store translated text or voice recordings.

4. Permissions

• Microphone: required for voice translation functionality.

5. Advertising and Third-Party Services

DuoVox displays advertisements provided by Google AdMob.

Google AdMob may collect and process device identifiers, advertising IDs, IP addresses, and other technical information to provide, personalize, and measure advertisements.

These data may be shared with Google and its advertising partners in accordance with their privacy policies.

6. Data Security

DuoVox does not store personal information on its own servers. Any data processed by third-party advertising providers is subject to their respective privacy and security practices.

7. Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

8. Contact

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact:

Email: romanellabs@gmail.com
