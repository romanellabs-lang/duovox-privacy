Privacy Policy – DuoVox

Last updated: 2026

DuoVox respects your privacy and is committed to protecting your personal data.

1. Data Collection

DuoVox does not collect, store, or share any personal data.

2. Audio Usage

The app uses your device's microphone only to convert speech into text. No audio is recorded, stored, or transmitted.

3. Translation

Translations are processed locally on your device whenever possible. No text or voice data is saved.

4. Permissions

- Microphone: required for voice translation functionality.

5. Data Security

Since DuoVox does not store any user data, there is no risk of data leakage.

This Privacy Policy may be updated in the future. Any changes will be reflected on this page.
